About Us

First, we understand the dynamics of the educational sector and the challenges faced by the schooling system, with major reference to COVID-19. That is why we are not afraid to think outside the box.

Virtual Learn Africa is a team of dedicated, hardworking and reliable educators, mentors, programmers and researchers who place learning above everything else. Formed in 2020 as a response to the "New Normal" the growing need for non-traditional learning that promotes self-discovery, knowledge construction, group-based discussions, transformational and independent learning, which is beneficial to students who want to exercise their flexibility and options on what to learn. We want to achieve a system of individual based acquisitions, using online learning contents, for the sole aim of achieving a desired career.

We employ the best teaching methods done online through our learning management system. Our modules are created in a friendly and engaging manner to capture the student's interest. We provide both free and paid learning resources provided by all learning platforms, for all career paths . We cultivate the passion of understanding the world around, giving us more motivation to impart learners.

Virtual Learn Africa provides flexible learning options for Africans using online resources. We cater to passionate individuals who need flexibility in their lives, yet still prioritize receiving a high-value education. Due to our adaptable study options, those enrolled at Virtual Learn Africa get the opportunity to progress through courses at the pace that works best for them. Along side online resources for every career path, we also provide mentorship and lecturer to student supervision for the sole aim of achieving a desired career.
